## User Stories
### Story 18.2: Analytics & Insights (21 points)
**User Story:** As a user, I want clear, deep analytics and plain-language insights that help me spot trends and act to improve my health faster.
#### Acceptance Criteria
- Trend/analytics dashboard shows clear, actionable outputs for weight, nutrition, activity, and more
- Correlation and prediction charts clearly explain possible causes/effects
- Weekly LLM natural-language reports highlight important developments, suggest next actions
- Export and share options easily available
- Handles: too little data, untrackable metrics, and edge cases with clear UX
- Automated and manual tests confirmed

# User Guide - Health Management App

## Overview

Welcome to the Health Management App! This guide will help you get started and make the most of all the features available in the app.

## Getting Started

### First Launch

**Welcome to Health Tracker!**

1. **Create Your Profile**
   - Enter your name and email
   - Set your date of birth
   - Enter your height
   - Set your target weight

2. **Set Your Preferences**
   - Choose your preferred units (metric/imperial)
   - Select your theme (light/dark)
   - Configure notification preferences

3. **Start Tracking**
   - Log your first weight entry
   - Log a meal
   - Add a medication (if applicable)
   - Create your first habit

## Feature Guides

### Health Tracking

**Tracking Your Weight**:
1. Navigate to Health Tracking
2. Tap "Add Weight"
3. Enter your weight
4. Select the date (defaults to today)
5. Tap "Save"

**Understanding Your 7-Day Average**:
- The 7-day moving average smooths out daily fluctuations
- Shows your weight trend more accurately
- Updated automatically when you add new weight entries
- Helps reduce anxiety from daily weight variations

**Tracking Sleep and Energy**:
1. Navigate to Health Tracking
2. Adjust the sleep quality slider (1-10)
3. Adjust the energy level slider (1-10)
4. Add optional notes
5. Tap "Save"

**Tracking Heart Rate and Blood Pressure**:
1. Navigate to Health Tracking
2. Enter your resting heart rate (BPM)
3. Enter your blood pressure (systolic/diastolic)
4. Add optional notes
5. Tap "Save"

**Taking Progress Photos**:
1. Navigate to Health Tracking > Progress Photos
2. Select photo type (Front, Side, Back)
3. Take photo in consistent lighting and clothing
4. Review and save
5. Photos are organized by month

### Nutrition Tracking

**Logging Your Meals**:
1. Navigate to Nutrition
2. Tap "Log Meal"
3. Select meal type (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snack)
4. Enter meal name
5. Enter macros (protein, fats, net carbs)
6. Calories are calculated automatically
7. Tap "Save"

**Understanding Your Macros**:
- **Protein**: Target 35% of calories (minimum)
- **Fats**: Target 55% of calories (minimum)
- **Net Carbs**: Maximum 40g per day
- Track daily totals to stay within targets

**Using Recipes**:
1. Navigate to Recipe Library
2. Search or browse recipes
3. Tap a recipe to view details
4. Tap "Log as Meal" to quickly log a recipe
5. Recipes show macros per serving

## Safety Features

### Understanding Safety Alerts

The app monitors your health metrics and alerts you to potential concerns. These alerts are designed to protect your health and should be taken seriously.

**Elevated Heart Rate Alert**:
- Triggered when resting heart rate > 100 BPM for 3 consecutive days
- Consult your healthcare provider if this persists
- May indicate dehydration, stress, or other health concerns

**Rapid Weight Loss Alert**:
- Triggered when weight loss > 1.8 kg (4 lbs) per week for 2 consecutive weeks
- Sustainable weight loss is 0.5-1 kg (1-2 lbs) per week
- Consult your healthcare provider

**Poor Sleep Alert**:
- Triggered when sleep quality < 4/10 for 5 consecutive days
- Review your sleep hygiene
- Consider consulting your healthcare provider

**Elevated Heart Rate from Baseline Alert**:
- Triggered when heart rate increases by > 20 BPM from baseline for 3 consecutive days
- Baseline is calculated from first 7 days of data
- Consult your healthcare provider if this persists

**Important**: Safety alerts cannot be permanently dismissed. They are designed to protect your health. Always consult with your healthcare provider about any health concerns.

## Data Management

### Exporting Your Data

1. Navigate to Settings
2. Tap "Export Data"
3. Review the confirmation dialog
4. Tap "Export"
5. The file will be saved to your device's Downloads folder (if accessible) or app storage
6. You can share the file to save it to cloud storage (Dropbox, Google Drive, etc.)

**Tips**:
- Export your data regularly (weekly recommended)
- Share exported files to cloud storage for safekeeping
- Keep multiple backup copies

### Importing Data

1. Navigate to Settings
2. Tap "Import Data"
3. Tap "Select Import File"
4. Use the file picker to browse and select your export file
   - Files in Downloads folder are easily accessible
   - Files in cloud storage (Dropbox, Google Drive) can be accessed through the file picker
5. The file will be validated automatically
6. Review the confirmation dialog (import will replace current data)
7. Tap "Import"
8. Wait for import to complete

**Important**: Importing data will replace your current data. Always export your current data first as a backup before importing.

**Troubleshooting Import**:
- If you can't find your file, use the file picker's navigation menu to browse folders
- Files shared to cloud storage can be accessed through the file picker
- Ensure the file is a valid export file (JSON format)

## Tips and Best Practices

### Accurate Tracking

- **Weigh yourself at the same time each day** (preferably in the morning, after using the bathroom)
- **Use consistent measurement conditions** (same scale, same time, same clothing)
- **Track regularly** for best results and trends
- **Be consistent** with your tracking routine

### Progress Photos

- Take photos in **consistent lighting**
- Wear **similar clothing** each time
- Take photos **monthly** for best comparison
- Photos help you see progress beyond the scale

### Medication Reminders

- **Set reminders** for all medications
- **Log doses** immediately after taking
- **Review medication logs** regularly
- **Track side effects** to share with your healthcare provider

### Staying Motivated

- **Focus on habits**, not just outcomes
- **Celebrate non-scale victories** (NSVs)
- **Review your progress** regularly
- **Adjust goals** as needed
- **Track multiple metrics** (not just weight)

### Nutrition Tracking

- **Log meals immediately** after eating for accuracy
- **Use recipes** to speed up meal logging
- **Review daily totals** to stay within macro targets
- **Plan meals ahead** when possible

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**App won't start**:
- Restart your device
- Clear app cache (Settings > Apps > Health Tracker > Storage > Clear Cache)
- Reinstall the app (your data is stored locally, but export first as backup)

**Notifications not working**:
- Check app notification settings (Settings > Notifications)
- Check device notification settings
- Ensure app is not in battery optimization
- Restart your device

**Data missing**:
- Check if you're looking at the correct date range
- Verify data was saved (check the list view)
- Export your data regularly as backup
- Check if you're filtering data incorrectly

**Chart not showing**:
- Ensure you have enough data points
- Check date range selection
- Try refreshing the screen
- Restart the app

**Export/Import not working**:
- Ensure you have storage permissions
- Try saving export file to Downloads folder
- For cloud storage, use the file picker's navigation menu
- Check that the file is a valid export file (JSON format)

**App running slowly**:
- Clear app cache
- Restart your device
- Check available storage space
- Update to latest app version

## Glossary

**7-Day Moving Average**: Average of weight over the last 7 days, used to smooth out daily fluctuations and show meaningful trends.

**Net Carbs**: Total carbohydrates minus fiber, used in low-carb/keto diets. Calculated as: Total Carbs - Fiber.

**NSV (Non-Scale Victory)**: Progress indicators beyond weight, such as increased energy, better sleep, improved mood, or clothing fitting better.

**Plateau**: Period when weight remains unchanged for an extended time (3+ weeks) despite continued efforts.

**Resting Heart Rate**: Heart rate when at rest, measured in beats per minute (BPM). Typically measured in the morning before getting out of bed.

**Macros**: Macronutrients - protein, fats, and carbohydrates. The app tracks these to help you meet your nutrition goals.

**Baseline**: Initial measurement used as a reference point. For heart rate, the baseline is the average of the first 7 days of data.

### ‚ú® Key Features

**Health Tracking**:
- Track weight with 7-day moving average for meaningful trends
- Log sleep quality and energy levels
- Monitor resting heart rate and blood pressure
- Take and organize progress photos
- View comprehensive health metrics dashboard

**Nutrition Management**:
- Log meals with macro tracking (protein, fats, net carbs)
- Track daily macro targets (35% protein, 55% fats, max 40g net carbs)
- Pre-populated recipe library with nutritional information
- Daily nutrition summary and progress tracking

**Exercise Tracking**:
- Log exercises and workouts
- Track duration, sets, reps, and weight
- Create and save workout plans
- Exercise history and progress tracking

**Medication Management**:
- Add medications with dosage and frequency
- Set up medication reminders
- Log medication doses
- Track medication history

**Habits & Goals**:
- Create and track daily habits
- Streak tracking for motivation
- Set and monitor goals (identity, behavior, outcome)
- Visual progress tracking

**Clinical Safety**:
- Automatic safety alerts for concerning health metrics:
  - Elevated heart rate alerts
  - Rapid weight loss warnings
  - Poor sleep quality alerts
  - Heart rate baseline deviation alerts
- Safety alerts cannot be permanently dismissed (for your protection)

**Data Management**:
- Export all your data to JSON file
- Import data from previous exports
- Local-only storage (your data stays on your device)
- Easy backup and restore functionality

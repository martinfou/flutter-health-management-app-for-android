# Play Store Listing Preparation

## App Information

### App Name
**Health Management App**

### Short Description (80 characters max)
**Comprehensive health tracking for weight loss, nutrition, exercise, and medications. Track your health journey.**

### Full Description (4000 characters max)

**Take control of your health journey with the Health Management App - your all-in-one companion for weight loss and holistic health management.**

**Track Everything in One Place**

Manage all aspects of your health with our comprehensive tracking features:

**Health Tracking**
- Monitor your weight with 7-day moving averages to see meaningful trends beyond daily fluctuations
- Track sleep quality and energy levels to understand your body's patterns
- Log resting heart rate and blood pressure
- Take progress photos to visualize your transformation
- View comprehensive health metrics on an intuitive dashboard

**Nutrition Management**
- Log meals with detailed macro tracking (protein, fats, net carbs)
- Stay within your daily targets: 35% protein, 55% fats, max 40g net carbs
- Access a pre-populated recipe library with nutritional information
- Track daily nutrition summary and progress

**Exercise & Movement**
- Log exercises and workouts with detailed tracking
- Record duration, sets, reps, and weight
- Create and save custom workout plans
- View exercise history and track progress over time

**Medication Management**
- Add medications with dosage and frequency information
- Set up medication reminders to never miss a dose
- Log medication doses and track history
- Monitor medication adherence

**Habits & Goals**
- Create and track daily habits with streak tracking
- Set and monitor goals (identity, behavior, outcome)
- Visual progress tracking to stay motivated
- Celebrate non-scale victories

**Clinical Safety Features**
- Automatic safety alerts for concerning health metrics
- Elevated heart rate warnings
- Rapid weight loss alerts
- Poor sleep quality notifications
- Heart rate baseline deviation alerts
- Safety alerts designed to protect your health

**Privacy & Data Control**
- All data stored locally on your device
- No cloud sync required (MVP version)
- Export and import your data anytime
- Complete control over your health information
- No account or login required

**Key Features:**
✅ Comprehensive health tracking
✅ 7-day moving averages for meaningful trends
✅ Macro tracking for nutrition goals
✅ Medication reminders
✅ Habit and goal tracking
✅ Clinical safety alerts
✅ Progress photos
✅ Data export/import
✅ Local-only storage (your data stays private)
✅ Beautiful, intuitive interface
✅ Accessibility compliant (WCAG 2.1 AA)

**Perfect For:**
- Weight loss journeys
- Health and wellness tracking
- Medication management
- Habit building
- Goal achievement
- Progress monitoring

**Privacy First:**
Your health data is important and private. That's why all data is stored locally on your device. No cloud sync, no accounts, no data sharing. You're in complete control.

**Start Your Health Journey Today!**

Download the Health Management App and take the first step toward better health. Track your progress, stay motivated, and achieve your health goals.

## Graphics Requirements

### App Icon
- **Size**: 512x512 pixels
- **Format**: PNG
- **Requirements**: 
  - No transparency
  - High quality
  - Represents app clearly
  - Follows Material Design guidelines

### Feature Graphic
- **Size**: 1024x500 pixels
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Requirements**:
  - Showcase key features
  - Include app name
  - High quality
  - Compelling visual

### Screenshots (Phone)
- **Minimum**: 2 screenshots
- **Maximum**: 8 screenshots
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 or 9:16
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Recommended Screenshots**:
  1. Home screen / Dashboard
  2. Health tracking (weight entry with chart)
  3. Nutrition logging
  4. Exercise tracking
  5. Medication management
  6. Habits and goals
  7. Progress photos
  8. Settings / Data export
